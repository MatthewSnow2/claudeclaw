DARPA's Air Combat Evolution program (2019-2024) achieved the first real AI vs human dogfight in an F-16 (X-62A VISTA). AlphaDogfight Trials (2020) had AI beat an experienced F-16 pilot in simulation. Not a public competition -- required DARPA contracts. Relevant because: it established the benchmark and proved RL can beat human pilots. AI Fight Club is Lockheed's commercial follow-on to this lineage.

HARD DISQUALIFIER: DARPA contractor program, not a public competition. Included for context only. The techniques (PPO, self-play, JSBSim) developed under ACE are what competitors in AI Fight Club will use.
