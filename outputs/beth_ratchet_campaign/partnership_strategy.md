## 4. Strategic Partnership Pitch

### The ROI Story for AccentCare/PointCare

**Frame: Ratchet is not a cost center. It's a retention and margin play.**

#### The Problem (with real numbers)

- Home health RN turnover rate: **27% (2024)**, down from 28% in 2023 but still elevated
- Home care aide turnover: **79.2% industry-wide** (catastrophic)
- Average cost to replace one staff RN: **$49,500 - $72,700** (Becker's Hospital Review, 2025)
- AccentCare has 30,000+ employees across 250+ locations
- Conservative estimate: AccentCare replaces 5,000-8,000 clinical staff per year
- **That's $250M-$580M in annual turnover costs**

#### The Documentation Problem

- Home health nurses spend **35-45% of their visit time on documentation**
- Nurses consistently cite documentation burden as a top reason for leaving home health
- Average visit documentation takes **45-60 minutes** after the visit
- Many nurses document at home in the evening -- unpaid time that drives burnout

#### What Ratchet Delivers

| Metric | Current State | With Ratchet | Impact |
|--------|--------------|--------------|--------|
| Documentation time per visit | 45-60 min | 10-15 min | **75% reduction** |
| Time to complete visit note | Same day or next day | Real-time during visit | Eliminates evening/weekend catch-up |
| Nurse satisfaction (documentation) | Major pain point | Resolved | Direct retention driver |
| Clinical note quality | Variable, fatigue-driven errors | Consistent AI-structured output | Better compliance, fewer audits |

#### The Financial Pitch

> "If Ratchet reduces nurse turnover by just 5 percentage points -- from 27% to 22% -- AccentCare saves approximately $50M-$100M annually in replacement costs alone. That doesn't count the productivity gains from faster documentation, reduced overtime, fewer compliance issues, or the competitive advantage of being the home health employer that nurses actually want to work for."

#### What M2AI Offers PointCare/AccentCare

1. **M2AI does the AI work.** AccentCare/HCHB gets the feature without building it.
2. **Ratchet is already built on PointCare.** Integration path is clear -- not theoretical.
3. **Zero risk pilot.** Start with 5-10 nurses in one market (Nashville). Measure time savings, nurse satisfaction, note quality. Data-driven go/no-go.
4. **Competitive moat.** If AccentCare/HCHB adopts Ratchet, they have AI documentation before every other home health EMR. Nurses will choose PointCare over competitors specifically for this feature.
5. **Revenue model flexibility.** Per-seat licensing, revenue share, or outright acquisition of the technology. M2AI is flexible on structure.

#### The Competitive Threat

Competitors are moving:
- **Roger Healthcare** (San Francisco, founded 2023): AI documentation for home health. Claims 80% time savings. Already on Google Play and App Store. Growing fast.
- **NurseMagic**: AI documentation assistant that works with any EHR via copy-paste.
- **Eleos Health**: AI documentation for behavioral health, expanding into adjacent verticals.

> "The question isn't whether AI documentation is coming to home health -- it's whether AccentCare leads or follows. Roger Healthcare is already selling to your competitors' nurses."
